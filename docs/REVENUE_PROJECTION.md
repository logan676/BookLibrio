## 3. Market Analysis

### 3.1 Target Audience

**Primary Demographics:**
- Age: 25-55 years old
- Location: Australia (primary), NZ, UK, USA (secondary)
- Characteristics: Avid readers, tech-savvy, value convenience

**Market Size (Australia):**
- Total population: ~26 million
- Adult population (18+): ~20 million
- Regular book readers: ~12 million (60%)
- E-reader/digital book users: ~4.8 million (40% of readers)
- Target addressable market: ~2.4 million (50% of digital readers)

### 3.2 Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Monthly Price | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------------|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Kindle Unlimited | $13.99 | Amazon ecosystem, huge library | Limited to Amazon |
| Audible | $16.45 | Quality audiobooks | 1 credit/month, expensive |
| Scribd | $14.99 | Multiple content types | Throttling system |
| BookLibrio | $9.99 | AI features, social reading | New entrant |

**Competitive Advantage:**
- AI-powered features (narration, Q&A, translation)
- Social reading and gamification
- Lower entry price point
- Modern, clean user experience

## 7. Profitability Analysis

### 7.1 Unit Economics

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):**
- Average subscription duration: 8-12 months
- LTV = ARPU × Average Lifespan
- Conservative LTV: $126 × 8 = **$1,008**
- Moderate LTV: $126 × 10 = **$1,260**
- Optimistic LTV: $126 × 12 = **$1,512**

**LTV:CAC Ratio:**
- Target: > 3:1 (healthy SaaS benchmark)
- Year 1: ~$1,008 / $10 = **100:1** (excellent)
- Year 2: ~$1,260 / $8 = **157:1** (excellent)
- Year 3: ~$1,512 / $6 = **252:1** (excellent)

### 7.2 Profit & Loss (Moderate Scenario)

| Item | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Revenue** | $700,000 | $2,455,000 | $6,300,000 |
| App Store Fees (20% avg) | ($140,000) | ($491,000) | ($1,260,000) |
| Payment Processing (3%) | ($21,000) | ($73,650) | ($189,000) |
| Cloud Infrastructure | ($28,800) | ($91,200) | ($250,800) |
| AI/TTS Variable Costs | ($37,500) | ($105,000) | ($225,000) |
| Marketing | ($100,000) | ($300,000) | ($600,000) |
| Personnel | ($150,000) | ($350,000) | ($700,000) |
| **Gross Profit** | **$222,700** | **$1,044,150** | **$3,075,200** |
| **Profit Margin** | **31.8%** | **42.5%** | **48.8%** |

### 7.3 Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly Break-Even Point:**
- Fixed Costs: ~$15,000/month (Year 1)
- Contribution Margin: ~$8/subscriber after platform fees
- Break-Even Subscribers: ~1,875/month

**Timeline to Profitability:**
- Conservative: Month 10-12
- Moderate: Month 6-8
- Optimistic: Month 4-6
